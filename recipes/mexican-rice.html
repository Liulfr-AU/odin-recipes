Mexican Rice

Description

An easy mexican rice to accompany any main dish!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 cup uncooked short-grain white rice
  • 1 and 3/4 cups chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees C
  2. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic; cook and stir until the onion is translucent.
  3. Stir in the chili powder and cumin, and cook for about 30 seconds.
  4. Add the rice, chicken broth and tomato paste, and bring to a boil. Transfer to a casserole dish, and cover with a lid.
  5. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes, or until liquid has been absorbed and rice is tender. Let rest for 3 to 5 minutes before serving.
