Pasta Bake

Description

A cheesy family favourite!

Ingredients

  • 500g mince (beef or pork)
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1 teaspoon basil
  • 500 grams pasta
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees C
  2. Heat a large frying pan over medium-high heat. Cook and stir ground beef in the hot pan until browned, for 5 to 7 minutes. Add tomato sauce, Parmesan cheese, garlic, oregano, and basil. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Stir in pasta, bring back to a boil, and cook pasta over medium heat until desired firmness, 8 to 12 minutes. Drain.
  4. Stir pasta into meat sauce, then spread into the baking dish. Sprinkle top with mozzarella cheese.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until the cheese has browned, 20 to 30 minutes.
