Squashed Beef Patties

Description

A quick and easy staple to get you through the week!

Ingredients

  • 500g mince
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Cut the mince into quarters.
  3. Place onto the grill and squash with a spatula. Cook for 12-15mins, flipping once.
  4. Sprinkle salt and pepper to taste.
